Understanding Manifests
A Manifest (farcaster.json) is required to register your Mini App and bind it to your domain. Think of it as DNS for your Mini App - it tells Farcaster clients about your app's name, icon, functionality, and enables deep integrations with the Farcaster ecosystem.
